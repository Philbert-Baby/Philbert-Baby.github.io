Me

A highly motivated and dedicated Data Analyst at Step5, leveraging analytical expertise and AI proficiency to uncover valuable insights from complex datasets. A First-Class Honours graduate in Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence, recognised for academic excellence and proficiency of multiple programming languages.

Hey there! I’m Philbert — a curious and driven data analyst who thrives at the intersection of numbers, tech, and people.

My journey into data began not with spreadsheets, but with conversations as a Market Researcher at MSX International, I learned how to ask the right questions and listen for insights buried between the lines. That role sparked my love for data-driven storytelling.

From there, I dove into the world of technology and innovation through a Technology Internship with Bright Network, gaining exposure to cutting-edge tools and frameworks that would later shape my approach as an analyst.

In July 2024, I joined the NHS as a Cancer Data Coordinator, where I worked on meaningful data that directly impacted healthcare decisions. Handling sensitive datasets taught me the importance of precision, privacy, and purpose in analytics.

Soon after, I stepped into my current role as a Graduate Data Analyst at Step5, a place where I get to combine everything I’ve learned: storytelling, technical acumen, and analytical rigour. Here, I design dashboards, scrape and structure messy data, and support clients in unlocking value through insight.

Alongside work, I’m proud to have earned a First Class Honours in Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence from Coventry University (2024), where I explored the ethical and practical sides of machine learning.

And I didn’t stop there. I’ve backed my experience with industry certifications, including:

  • Microsoft Certified: Data Analyst (Power BI)
  • Career Essentials in Data Analysis
  • Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)

To me, data isn’t just numbers, it’s a narrative. And I love helping teams turn data into decisions, and insights into impact.
